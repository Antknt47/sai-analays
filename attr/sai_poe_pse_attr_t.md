- **Type**: sai_uint32_t

- **Brief**: POE device ID
- **Type**: sai_object_id_t
- **Flags**: MANDATORY_ON_CREATE | CREATE_ONLY
- **Objects**: SAI_OBJECT_TYPE_POE_DEVICE
